# PRODUCTION NOTES

## Key Visual Elements
- **Color Palette:** Deep blacks, emerald green (#10b981), soft white glows
- **Typography:** Bold, modern sans-serif for headlines; clean body text
- **Motion:** Smooth transitions, subtle particle effects for Web3/blockchain moments
- **Energy:** Builds from calm/contemplative to inspiring/empowering

## Key Stats to Highlight (Optional B-Roll Text Overlays)
- 2% marketplace fees
- 9 smart contracts
- 24/7 AI moderation
- NFT shop ownership
- Instant AXM payments

## Voice Characteristics
- Warm, confident, and genuine
- Not aggressive or "crypto bro"
- Speaks to the heart, not just the wallet
- Gender-neutral casting recommended

## Music Suggestions
- Modern electronic with organic elements
- Builds gradually, peaks at marketplace/academy reveal
- Triumphant but not overwhelming at the close
- Royalty-free options: Epidemic Sound, Artlist

## Alternative Short Version (30 seconds)
Use Scenes 1, 3, 5, and 8 for a condensed social media cut.
